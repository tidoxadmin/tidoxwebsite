Our Mission

We believe your digital life should be private, simple, and under your control. Tidox apps are built with privacy-first principles, giving you powerful tools without compromising your data.

Privacy First

Simple & Powerful

User Control

You own your data. Export, delete, or modify anything at any time.

Our Story

Tidox started with a simple observation: most mobile apps collect far more data than they need. We wondered if it was possible to build powerful, useful apps that respected user privacy from the ground up.

The answer was yes. By processing everything on-device and never requiring cloud sync, we created apps that are both more private and more reliable. No internet connection? No problem. Concerned about data breaches? Your data never leaves your device.

Today, Tidox offers three focused apps: Player for streaming media, Transcript for private audio transcription, and Task Manager for simple project organization. Each app does one thing exceptionally well, without the bloat or privacy concerns of traditional alternatives.
