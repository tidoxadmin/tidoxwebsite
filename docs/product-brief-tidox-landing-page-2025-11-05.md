# Product Brief: Tidox Landing Page Redesign

**Date:** 2025-11-05
**Author:** etido
**Context:** Business - Multi-Product Portfolio Transformation

---

## Executive Summary

Tidox is transitioning from a single-product landing page (Tidox Player only) to a comprehensive multi-product portfolio website showcasing three innovative mobile applications. This redesign addresses the critical need to establish Tidox as a credible, modern mobile app development company in 2025, capable of attracting users across diverse product categories while converting free users to premium tiers.

**The Challenge:** Current website (tidox.online) focuses exclusively on Tidox Player, leaving Offline Transcript and Task Manager invisible to potential users. This single-product presentation undermines company credibility and misses cross-product opportunities.

**The Solution:** A conversion-optimized, multi-product showcase website built on Framer, featuring modern 2025 design aesthetics, unified pricing transparency, and strategic messaging that positions Tidox's privacy-first, innovation-focused approach.

**Success Metrics:**
- Unified brand presence across 3 products
- Clear freemium-to-premium conversion pathways
- Mobile-first experience (62.54% of traffic is mobile)
- 4-week timeline from design to launch

**Market Opportunity:** Competitive research reveals significant differentiation opportunities—particularly Offline Transcript's privacy-first positioning against cloud-dependent competitors like Otter.ai. Website redesign enables proper communication of these advantages.

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

**Current Reality:**
Tidox has evolved from a single-product company to a multi-product mobile app portfolio, but the website hasn't kept pace. Visitors to tidox.online see only Tidox Player, remaining unaware of Offline Transcript (AI transcription) and Task Manager (launching soon). This creates multiple problems:

1. **Credibility Gap**: Single-product website makes Tidox appear as a niche player, not a capable mobile app development company
2. **Missed Discovery**: Users seeking transcription or task management solutions never find Tidox's offerings
3. **Lost Cross-Product Synergy**: Player users don't discover Transcript; Transcript users don't know about Player
4. **Conversion Friction**: Lack of unified pricing page makes freemium-to-premium decision difficult
5. **SEO Limitations**: No dedicated landing pages for each product means lost organic search traffic
6. **Outdated Aesthetics**: Current design doesn't reflect 2025 web design standards, undermining trust

**Who's Affected:**
- **Potential Users**: Can't discover products that solve their problems
- **Current Users**: Miss opportunities to explore additional Tidox products
- **Business**: Lost downloads, premium conversions, and market positioning

### Problem Impact

**Quantifiable Impact:**

**Discovery/Traffic Loss:**
- Offline Transcript has ZERO website presence despite being live on Play Store
- Task Manager launch will have no website support
- Missing product-specific SEO traffic (e.g., "offline transcription app", "IPTV player Chromecast")

**Conversion Impact:**
- Research shows transparent pricing pages achieve 4.6% conversion vs 2.8% without transparency
- Current site lacks unified pricing—impossible to compare free vs premium across products
- Mobile users (62.54% of traffic) face poor mobile experience
- 40% of users abandon sites with poor mobile UX (per research)

**Competitive Disadvantage:**
- Offline Transcript's privacy-first advantage (offline processing) is invisible to market
- Can't compete with Otter.ai, VLC, Todoist on website credibility
- Established players have polished multi-product presences (researched: Atlassian, Asana, Notion)

**Strategic Cost:**
- Company appears less mature/capable than reality
- Difficult to attract partnerships or press coverage
- Task Manager launch will lack proper marketing foundation

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

**Option 1: Keep Current Single-Product Site**
- ❌ Doesn't scale with product portfolio growth
- ❌ Continues credibility and discovery problems
- ❌ No unified brand presence

**Option 2: Simple Product Listing Page**
- ❌ Lacks strategic positioning and storytelling
- ❌ Doesn't address freemium conversion optimization
- ❌ Misses 2025 design trends that build trust

**Option 3: Off-the-Shelf Website Templates**
- ❌ Generic aesthetics don't differentiate
- ❌ Not optimized for multi-product app companies
- ❌ Limited customization for Tidox's unique value props

**What's Needed:**
A purpose-built, conversion-optimized multi-product portfolio website that:
- Establishes company credibility through professional, modern design
- Creates clear discovery pathways for each product
- Implements freemium pricing best practices with transparency
- Positions competitive advantages (especially privacy-first transcription)
- Supports future product launches without redesigns
- Embraces 2025 design trends that mobile app users expect

### Proposed Solution

**Vision: Tidox Unified Portfolio Website**

A modern, mobile-first website that transforms tidox.online from a single-product landing page into a comprehensive showcase of Tidox's innovative mobile app ecosystem—positioning the company as a credible leader in privacy-first, modern mobile solutions.

**Core Experience:**

**Homepage - Company-First Positioning:**

## Technical Preferences

### Platform Selection: Framer

**Decision Rationale (Research-Backed):**

**Framer Selected ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)**

**Reasons:**
1. **Design Freedom**: Figma-like interface enables 2025 aesthetic trends (micro-interactions, animations, modern layouts)
2. **Speed to Market**: 4-week timeline achievable with visual development
3. **Sufficient Functionality**: CMS adequate for 3-product portfolio (not 100+ items)
4. **Cost-Effective**: $15/month vs Webflow $12+ (scales better for needs)
5. **React-Based**: Modern stack (React SSR) provides performance without custom coding
6. **Easy Updates**: Active product development requires frequent updates—Framer editing is fast
7. **Perfect Use Case**: Product showcases and portfolios are Framer's sweet spot

**Alternatives Considered:**

**Webflow (⭐⭐⭐ 3/5):**
- Pros: Strong CMS (10K items), excellent SEO, robust e-commerce
- Cons: Steeper learning curve, overkill for 3 products, less design freedom for animations
- **Why Not**: Unnecessary complexity for portfolio site, longer development time

**Next.js (⭐⭐⭐ 3/5):**
- Pros: Maximum control, performance, scalability
- Cons: Requires React/Node.js expertise, slower to market, higher maintenance
- **Why Not**: Development resources required, overkill for marketing site

**Framer is the optimal choice** for Tidox's specific needs: fast launch, design excellence, adequate functionality, cost-effective.
