Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download the apps?

All Tidox apps are available on the Google Play Store. Visit our pricing page for download links.

Are the apps really privacy-focused?

Yes! We don't collect personal data, no cloud sync required, and all processing happens on your device.

What's the difference between free and premium?

Free versions have essential features. Premium unlocks advanced functionality like unlimited playlists, batch transcription, and more.

Can I use the apps offline?

Absolutely! All Tidox apps work completely offline. No internet connection required after download.
